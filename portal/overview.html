Pillar 04 — Client Portal

Introducing the Sachsen
Client Portal

A secure, self-service digital platform that gives your clients 24/7 visibility into their shipments, documents, invoices, and communications — all in one place.

See the Solution View Live Demo
The Problem

The Challenge

In today's logistics landscape, clients expect instant access to information. The current manual workflow creates friction for everyone involved.

📞

Constant Follow-Ups

Clients call and email for every status update, tying up your team's time with routine inquiries that could be self-service.

📄

Documents Get Lost

Bills of lading, invoices, and certificates are scattered across emails, WhatsApp messages, and physical files — hard to find when needed.

📈

Invoice Tracking Gaps

Clients struggle to track outstanding payments, and your finance team spends hours reconciling and chasing late invoices manually.

The Solution

24/7 Self-Service Portal

A branded client portal that puts every piece of information at your clients' fingertips — reducing calls, improving satisfaction, and positioning Sachsen as a modern logistics partner.

🚢

Real-Time Tracking

Live shipment tracking with visual timelines, vessel details, ETAs, and milestone notifications for every consignment.

📋

Instant Quotes

Clients submit quote requests online, receive estimates, and approve — all without a single phone call.

📁

Document Vault

Centralized, searchable repository for all shipping documents — BOLs, invoices, packing lists, certificates — always available.

💰

Invoice Management

View, download, and track payment status for all invoices. Automated reminders reduce late payments.

💬

Secure Messaging

Threaded conversations linked to specific shipments. Full audit trail replaces scattered emails and WhatsApp messages.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Automated email and in-app alerts for milestones — customs clearance, arrival, delivery — so clients never need to ask.

Service Overview

Comprehensive Sea Freight Management

As one of Tanzania's leading clearing and forwarding agents, Sachsen Spedition manages the full lifecycle of your ocean shipments. From booking vessel space with major shipping lines to coordinating customs clearance at the Port of Dar es Salaam, we ensure your cargo arrives on schedule and within budget.

Our sea freight team maintains direct relationships with all major carriers calling at Dar es Salaam, giving us access to competitive rates, priority booking slots, and real-time vessel tracking. Whether you are importing manufacturing inputs from Asia or exporting agricultural products to Europe, we have the expertise to handle it.

We specialize in both full container load (FCL) shipments for large volumes and less-than-container load (LCL) consolidation for smaller cargo, giving businesses of every size access to efficient ocean freight services.

Capabilities

Sea Freight Features

  • ⚓

    FCL & LCL Shipments

    Full container loads for high-volume cargo and consolidated less-than-container loads for cost-effective smaller shipments. We manage both 20ft and 40ft containers in standard, high-cube, refrigerated, and open-top configurations.

  • 📋

    Dar es Salaam Port Expertise

    Deep operational knowledge of the Port of Dar es Salaam, including TICTS terminal operations, container handling procedures, and port authority protocols. We minimize dwell times and avoid unnecessary storage charges.

  • ⚖

    Customs Clearance

    Integrated customs brokerage services ensure your cargo clears the port without delays. Our licensed brokers handle TRA declarations, duty assessment, and all regulatory inspections on your behalf.

  • 📍

    Container Tracking

    Real-time visibility into your container's location from origin port to Dar es Salaam and onward to final delivery. Proactive status updates and alerts keep you informed at every stage of the voyage.

  • 📦

    Consolidated Cargo

    For businesses shipping smaller volumes, our LCL consolidation service groups your cargo with other shipments to reduce costs while maintaining transit reliability and security.

  • 🚚

    Port-to-Door Delivery

    Seamless last-mile logistics from the Port of Dar es Salaam to your warehouse, factory, or project site anywhere in Tanzania. We coordinate trucking and inland transport to complete the supply chain.

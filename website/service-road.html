Service Overview

The Road Network That Drives East African Trade

The Port of Dar es Salaam serves as the primary gateway for landlocked nations in the region. Sachsen Spedition operates a robust road transport service that connects the port to destinations across East and Central Africa, ensuring cargo reaches even the most remote locations safely and on schedule.

Our road transport division manages the entire overland journey, from port gate-out to border crossing and final delivery. We handle all transit documentation, customs bonds, border clearance formalities, and regulatory compliance required for cross-border trucking in the region.

With a network of vetted and GPS-tracked transporters, we provide full visibility over your cargo's journey. Our operations team monitors every truck in real-time, proactively managing delays and ensuring your supply chain stays on track.

Capabilities

Road Transport Features

  • 🌎

    Cross-Border Trucking

    End-to-end road freight from Dar es Salaam to destinations in Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, Burundi, DRC, Malawi, and Zambia. We manage all border formalities, transit bonds, and regulatory paperwork at every crossing point.

  • 🚚

    Full Truck Loads (FTL)

    Dedicated trucks for large shipments requiring exclusive vehicle use. Available in flatbed, curtain-sided, enclosed, and tanker configurations to match your cargo type and volume requirements.

  • 📦

    Less-Than-Truckload (LTL)

    Cost-effective shared transport for smaller shipments. We consolidate cargo from multiple clients onto a single vehicle, reducing per-unit transport costs without compromising delivery reliability.

  • 📄

    Transit Permits & Bonds

    Expert management of TANCIS transit documentation, customs bonds, and corridor-specific permits required for moving goods through Tanzania and across regional borders under EAC protocols.

  • ⚖

    Border Clearance

    Dedicated agents at key border posts including Namanga, Holili, Rusumo, Kabanga, Tunduma, and Kasumulu ensure swift processing and minimal waiting times at every crossing point.

  • 📍

    GPS Tracking

    Every vehicle in our network is equipped with GPS tracking. Our operations center monitors truck locations 24/7, providing real-time position updates and estimated arrival times to keep you informed throughout transit.

Regional Coverage

Major Trade Routes

Our road network covers the primary trade corridors connecting Tanzania to its regional partners.

🇰🇪 Kenya

Dar es Salaam to Nairobi via Namanga or Holili/Taveta border. Transit time: 2-3 days. Key sectors: FMCG, manufacturing, electronics.

🇺🇬 Uganda

Dar es Salaam to Kampala via Mutukula border. Transit time: 3-4 days. Key sectors: construction materials, fuel, agricultural inputs.

🇷🇼 Rwanda

Dar es Salaam to Kigali via Rusumo border. Transit time: 3-4 days. Key sectors: mining equipment, consumer goods, construction.

🇨🇩 DR Congo

Dar es Salaam to Lubumbashi and eastern DRC via Kabanga or Kasumulu. Transit time: 5-7 days. Key sectors: mining, fuel, heavy machinery.

🇧🇮 Burundi

Dar es Salaam to Bujumbura via Kabanga border. Transit time: 3-4 days. Key sectors: food products, building materials, fuel.

🇲🇼 Malawi & 🇿🇲 Zambia

Dar es Salaam to Lilongwe and Lusaka via Tunduma/Kasumulu. Transit time: 3-5 days. Key sectors: agriculture, mining, industrial supplies.

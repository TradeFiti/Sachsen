Our Story

About Sachsen Spedition

A legacy of excellence in East African logistics, built on trust, precision, and an unwavering commitment to our clients' success.

Founded in Dar es Salaam

Powering Trade Across East Africa

Sachsen Spedition (T) Limited was established in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, with a clear mission: to simplify the complexities of international trade for businesses operating in and through East Africa.

From our offices on Samora/Zanaki Street, in the heart of Dar es Salaam's commercial district, we have grown from a small customs brokerage firm into a full-service clearing and forwarding company handling thousands of shipments annually.

Our deep understanding of the Tanzanian regulatory environment, combined with a network of global shipping partners, positions us uniquely to serve importers, exporters, and multinational corporations who demand reliability and compliance in every transaction.

Purpose & Direction

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional clearing and forwarding services that empower businesses to trade confidently across borders. We achieve this through deep regulatory expertise, transparent pricing, and an unrelenting focus on timely execution.

Every shipment we handle reflects our commitment to precision, compliance, and the economic growth of Tanzania and the broader East African community.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and technologically advanced logistics partner in East Africa, setting the standard for how clearing and forwarding services are delivered in the region.

We envision a future where every business, from emerging enterprises to established multinationals, has access to world-class logistics support that makes cross-border trade seamless, transparent, and efficient.

Service Overview

Air Freight for Time-Critical Cargo

Sachsen Spedition's air freight division handles the most demanding shipping requirements with precision and urgency. Whether you need to rush spare parts to a mining operation, deliver perishable agricultural products to European markets, or import critical medical supplies, our team ensures your cargo flies on schedule.

We work with all major airlines operating through Julius Nyerere International Airport (JNIA), including Ethiopian Airlines, Turkish Cargo, Emirates SkyCargo, Kenya Airways, and Qatar Airways Cargo. This extensive carrier network gives us flexibility to find the fastest, most cost-effective routing for every shipment.

From pickup to customs clearance to final delivery, we manage every touchpoint of the air freight process, including specialized handling for temperature-sensitive, hazardous, and oversized cargo.

Air Freight Features

  • ⚡

    Express Cargo

    Priority handling for urgent shipments that need to arrive within 24 to 48 hours. We secure next-available-flight bookings and expedite customs clearance to minimize transit time from origin to your doorstep.

  • 📦

    Standard Air Freight

    Economical air cargo services for shipments where speed is important but a few extra days are acceptable. We consolidate cargo to achieve the best rates while maintaining reliable transit schedules.

  • ✈

    JNIA Handling

    Expert management of all cargo operations at Julius Nyerere International Airport, including warehouse coordination, security screening, customs inspection, and carrier liaison for both inbound and outbound shipments.

  • ❄

    Perishable Goods

    Temperature-controlled handling for flowers, fresh produce, seafood, pharmaceuticals, and other perishable commodities. We coordinate cold-chain logistics from origin to final destination to preserve product integrity.

  • ⚠

    Dangerous Goods

    Certified handling and documentation for IATA-classified dangerous goods including chemicals, batteries, and industrial materials. Our team is trained in DG regulations and ensures full compliance with aviation safety standards.

  • 🚚

    Door-to-Airport & Airport-to-Door

    Complete pickup and delivery services connecting your premises to the airport and from JNIA to any location in Tanzania. We coordinate ground transport on both ends for a seamless door-to-door experience.

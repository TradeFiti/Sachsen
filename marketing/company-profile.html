About Us

Sachsen Spedition (T) Limited is a premier clearing and forwarding company headquartered in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. With over 15 years of experience navigating the complexities of international trade logistics, we provide end-to-end supply chain solutions that connect East African businesses to global markets.

Our dedicated team of licensed customs brokers and logistics professionals ensures seamless cargo movement across sea, air, and road networks — delivering reliability, transparency, and speed at every stage.
